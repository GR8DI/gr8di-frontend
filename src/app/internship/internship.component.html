Summer Internship

♦

The groovy Summer Internship organised by GR8DI, is a program initiated to help students in the English speaking part of Cameroon to complete their degree program.Due to the increased violence in Cameroon, several of our students have been left without summer internships. To help solve this issue, GR8DI with the help of Jennifer Strater and Kamwa Fosso Arcel decided to organise Summer Internships for students to learn and build the GR8DI website. The first badge of this program mainly consisted of students of the College Of Technology (University Of Buea). These students will be mentored by Kamwa Fosso Arcel Raulain (aka kraulain).
