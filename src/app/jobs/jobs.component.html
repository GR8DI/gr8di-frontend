“Talent is equally distributed around the world… opportunity is not”

The GR8DI initiative helps pair underrepresented minorities with great companies. Companies can post job advertisements or links to other sites for free. Please note that most potential applications are junior level (after having completed a high quality Groovy training course) and seeking remote work or work in their home countries. If you offer relocation assistance/visa sponsorship/etc please note that in the listing. New postings will be sent to potential applicants via email until expired. By default, the post will expire after 30 days, but can be renewed if the position is still open.
